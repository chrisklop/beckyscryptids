Job 41 describes Leviathan in language so vivid it sounds like a sighting report: *"his sneezings flash forth light, and his eyes are like the eyelids of the morning… by his neesings a light doth shine."* The creature laughs at spears. It cannot be hauled out with a hook. It is sovereign of the deep.

Christian commentators argued for centuries about whether Leviathan was crocodile, whale, satan, or a real sea beast. Each interpretation won for a century, then lost. The certainty came back every time some sailor came home with a story too big for the harbor.

The deep, after all, is mostly unmapped. Anything can still be down there. Becky thinks something is.
